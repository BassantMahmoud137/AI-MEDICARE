    Diagnosis

    Diagnosis is the identification of a disease or condition based on evaluation of symptoms, medical tests, and other relevant information.

    Consultation

    Consultation is a professional interaction between a healthcare provider and a patient, where advice, opinions, or medical guidance are sought and provided to address a specific health concern or condition.

    Tracking symptoms involves systematically recording and monitoring specific signs or indications of a health condition over time to better understand patterns, progression, and potential triggers.
